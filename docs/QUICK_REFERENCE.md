## **💡 How To Use (Quick Guide)**

### For Desktop:

1. **Start Page**
   - Fill visitor details in Step 1
   - Watch badge preview update live on right →

2. **Step 2 - Camera**
   - Click big blue "**Activate Camera**" button
   - Use controls: [↔️] [⚡] [+] [-]
   - Click "**Capture Photo**" when ready
   - See flash effect + toast "✓ Photo captured!"
   - Click "**Retake**" if needed
   - Click "**Stop Camera**" to save battery

3. **Complete**
   - Fill remaining fields
   - Sign in signature pad
   - Check terms checkbox
   - Click "**Complete Registration**"

### For Mobile:

1. **Bottom Nav Always There**
   - Tap 💾 to save draft anytime
   - Tap 🏠 to go home
   - Tap 🕐 to load draft

2. **Use FAB (Blue Button)**
   - Tap to go to next step
   - On step 3, tap to submit

3. **Swipe To Navigate**
   - Swipe left for next
   - Swipe right for previous

4. **Pull Down To Reload**
   - At top of page, pull down
   - Releases → loads draft

5. **Watch Step Dots**
   - 3 dots at top show progress
   - Active dot grows
