Visitor Code of Conduct

1. Identification: All visitors must wear their issued badge at all times while on the premises.

2. Escort Policy: Visitors must be accompanied by their host at all times unless authorized otherwise.

3. Recording Devices: The use of cameras, video, or audio recording equipment is strictly prohibited without prior authorization.

4. Confidentiality: Visitors agree to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary information seen or heard during the visit.

5. Emergency Procedures: In the event of an alarm, please follow the instructions of your host and proceed to the nearest emergency assembly point.

6. Workplace Safety: Visitors must comply with all posted safety signs and use required personal protective equipment (PPE) in designated areas.
